(and another one is <a href="http://www.mozilla.or.id/space.html" data-href="http://www.mozilla.or.id/space.html" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Jakarta Space</a>). These spaces are funded by the Mozilla Participation Team, and managed by local Mozillian volunteers.</p><p name="cefe" id="cefe" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Here, I will share some brief status of the Taipei Space.</p><p name="eb5a" id="eb5a" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">(I’ll use “MozTW Space” or “Space” as the abbreviation for “Mozilla Community Space Taipei” below.)</p><p name="28a2" id="28a2" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">摩茲工寮是 Mozilla 與開放原始碼、開放資料、開放文化，以及公民駭客的基地。台北的摩茲工寮，是首個 Mozilla 資助成立的志工空間，現行另一個 Mozilla Community Space 在印尼的雅加達。</p><p name="4597" id="4597" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">以下我將會分享摩茲工寮至今的相關數據。</p></div></div></section><section name="968e" class="section section--body"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--outsetColumn"><figure name="aacb" id="aacb" class="graf graf--figure graf--layoutOutsetCenter graf--leading"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*4RqFBUHOb6n0gv8TQsMKsw.jpeg" data-width="2048" data-height="1536" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/1*4RqFBUHOb6n0gv8TQsMKsw.jpeg"></figure></div><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 name="9ba5" id="9ba5" class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--figure">MozTW, Mozilla 台灣社群 (Mozilla Taiwan Community)</h3><p name="7426" id="7426" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h3">Mozilla Taiwan Community (MozTW) is a local Mozilla volunteer community. We have contributed to localizing, promoting Firefox, Mozilla, and open cultures to Mozilla and open source projects since 2004.</p><p name="4a38" id="4a38" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">MozTW 是在地的 Mozilla 志工社群。我們自 2004 年持續在翻譯及推廣 Mozilla、Firefox 及開放文化上貢獻至今。</p><p name="12b0" id="12b0" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">MozTW maintained an online <a href="https://moztw.org/" data-href="https://moztw.org/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">product portal and forum</a>, hosting <a href="https://moztw.org/events/" data-href="https://moztw.org/events/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">many offline events</a> and engaging with other Asia Mozillians and open-source hackers.</p><p name="141e" id="141e" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">MozTW 的主要任務是維護線上的 <a href="https://moztw.org/" data-href="https://moztw.org/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener noopener" target="_blank">Firefox 產品網站及討論區</a> 及舉辦 <a href="https://moztw.org/events/" data-href="https://moztw.org/events/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener noopener" target="_blank">線下活動</a> 與其他 Mozillians 與開放文化志工與駭客交流。</p></div></div></section><section name="bc41" class="section section--body"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 name="7fb4" id="7fb4" class="graf graf--h3 graf--leading">摩茲工寮簡史 History of MozTW Community Space</h3><p name="b9b4" id="b9b4" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h3">The Community Space was initially raised by William Quiviger in 2013, and sponsored by the WPR team. We started the first experimental space in Taipei in the spring of 2014.</p><p name="5693" id="5693" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">After April 2016, the space project transitioned to the Participation Team, supported by Brian King. In February 2017, it was continuous with Open Innovation Group (later Community Team) with the help of George Roter and Konstantina until now.</p><p name="6f70" id="6f70" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Community Space 社群空間專案最早由 William Quiviger 於 2013 年發起，台北作為第一個實驗空間，於 2014 年春天成立。</p></div><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--outsetColumn"><figure name="b532" id="b532" class="graf graf--figure graf--layoutOutsetCenter graf-after--p"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*JIc_a3WmzYCj4wBOeZE_xw.jpeg" data-width="2048" data-height="1536" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/1*JIc_a3WmzYCj4wBOeZE_xw.jpeg"></figure></div><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 name="0cdf" id="0cdf" class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--figure">Keyholders</h3><p name="75c0" id="75c0" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h3">MozTW Space is co-managing by “Keyholders,” 14 local Mozilla volunteers.</p><p name="a214" id="a214" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Keyholders are all vouched Mozillians and have the same privilege. When they apply for the position, people need to be countersigned by more than a certain percentage of Mozillian visitors (with a community profile and having visited 3 times in the last 3 months). The threshold represents the community’s support to the applicant.</p><p name="fb0c" id="fb0c" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">摩茲工寮由 14 位「Keyholder」本地志工共同管理。Keyholder 經 Mozilla community profile 的工寮使用者投票以取得資格。Keyholder 具有同等的權限，可以自由進出與協助其他志工與社群使用工寮空間。</p></div></div></section><section name="70a3" class="section section--body"><div class="section-divider"><hr class="section-divider"></div><div class="section-content"><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--outsetColumn"><figure name="7dbe" id="7dbe" class="graf graf--figure graf--layoutOutsetCenter graf--leading"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*xnvUFSANV4Y2a0U5dhI1lw.jpeg" data-width="2048" data-height="1536" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/1*xnvUFSANV4Y2a0U5dhI1lw.jpeg"></figure></div><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><h3 name="43f1" id="43f1" class="graf graf--h3 graf-after--figure">使用者與活動 Visitors &amp; Events</h3><p name="fa17" id="fa17" class="graf graf--p graf-after--h3">Everyone is welcome to visit the MozTW space when there is any Keyholder inside. However, it’s not opening 24 hours / seven days a week due to the volunteers’ ability. In the last five years, we managed to open the space 19 days per month on average. (Part of the weeknight and most of the time on the weekend)</p><p name="19bc" id="19bc" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Besides Mozilla events, we also welcome and support all open culture communities to host their activities in the MozTW Space. More than <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">23,000 visitors</strong> and <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">2,000 events/meetups</strong> from <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">77 open communities </strong>since the soft opening of May 2014.</p><p name="c9ea" id="c9ea" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">工寮於任何活動舉行時、與 keyholder 在內時均開放，歡迎任何人自由參觀與使用（開放狀態公布於網站行事曆與 MozTW Telegram 頻道）。受限於志工的能量，我們並未每週七天均開放。統計過去九年間，工寮平均每月開放 <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">19 天</strong>。（大部分的週末白天及部分週間晚上時段）</p><p name="88e9" id="88e9" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">除了 Mozilla 自己的活動以外，我們也支持所有開放文化社群在此舉辦活動與聚會。自 2014 年五月開幕至今，包含 MozTW 在內，總計有 <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">77</strong> 個開放社群，共在此舉辦了超過<strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">兩千場</strong>活動與聚會，共有超過<strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">兩萬三千名</strong>參加者與訪客。</p><p name="d87d" id="d87d" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">The communities that hosted the events at space came from diverse domains, including open source projects, programming, AI, blockchain, policy advocacy, civil society hackers, CS education, open culture, open data, DEI, infoSec, makers &amp; designers.</p><p name="e516" id="e516" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Some of the closest cooperate communities include Rust Taiwan, <a href="http://g0v.tw/" data-href="http://g0v.tw/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">G0V</a>, <a href="http://openstreetmap.tw/" data-href="http://openstreetmap.tw/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">OSM</a>, <a href="https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Wikimedia_Taiwan" data-href="https://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Wikimedia_Taiwan" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Wikimedia Taiwan</a>, <a href="http://creativecommons.tw/%3Cbr%3E" data-href="http://creativecommons.tw/%3Cbr%3E" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Creative Commons Taiwan</a>, <a href="https://www.ubuntu-tw.org/" data-href="https://www.ubuntu-tw.org/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Ubuntu TW</a>, <a href="http://ocf.tw/" data-href="http://ocf.tw/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Open Culture Foundation</a>, <a href="http://sitcon.org/" data-href="http://sitcon.org/" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">SITCON</a> (Students Information Technology Conference), and <a href="http://coscup.org" data-href="http://coscup.org" class="markup--anchor markup--p-anchor" rel="noopener ugc nofollow noopener" target="_blank">COSCUP</a> (Conference for Open Source Coders, Users &amp; Promoters).</p><p name="d774" id="d774" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">在此舉辦活動的開放社群來自非常多領域，包含開放原始碼、程式、至人工智慧、區塊鍊、網路與隱私政策倡議、公民科技與駭客、資訊教育、開放文化、開放資料、多元共融、資安、設計與 Maker 社群等等。</p></div><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--outsetColumn"><figure name="ea68" id="ea68" class="graf graf--figure graf--layoutOutsetCenter graf-after--p"><img class="graf-image" data-image-id="1*Azm0AQ5yHUwc_c2a1k6EEA.jpeg" data-width="2048" data-height="1536" src="https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/1*Azm0AQ5yHUwc_c2a1k6EEA.jpeg"></figure></div><div class="section-inner sectionLayout--insetColumn"><p name="43d1" id="43d1" class="graf graf--p graf-after--figure">One of the primary goals is to introduce Mozilla and Open Culture to new friends. In last year (July 2022 — June 2023), more than <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">260 new visitors</strong> had learned about Open Source on their first visit to space (out of <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">1600 visitors</strong> in <strong class="markup--strong markup--p-strong">183 events</strong>).</p><p name="c50d" id="c50d" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p">Not only Mozilla, we also introduce Internet Freedom, Privacy, Digital Policy, Multi-Stackholder Governance, Open Source, Open Data, Open Governance, Public License…all different technologies and ideas around the Internet.</p><p name="aead" id="aead" class="graf graf--p graf-after--p graf--trailing">工寮的其中一個主要目標是介紹 Mozilla 與開放文化給新朋友。在過去一年間（2022 年中至 2023 年中）到訪工寮的 1600 位訪客中，有 260 位新訪客首次接觸 Mozilla、網路自由、網路隱私、數位政策、多方關係人治理、開放原始碼、開放資料、開放政府、開放授權等各式各樣的理念。</p></div></div></section><section name="afe4" class="section section--body"><div class="section-divider"><hr
